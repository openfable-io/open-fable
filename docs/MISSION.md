# OpenFable — Mission Statement

**Verified output. Open to everyone.**

*June 2026*

---

## Why OpenFable

AI output should be trustworthy by default. Not because a corporation guarantees it — because the process itself is verifiable.

The current landscape offers two extremes: enterprise solutions locked behind contracts and pricing, or raw open-source models with no quality layer. Neither serves the people who actually build — the solo developers, the small teams, the creators who just need AI that works without a sales call.

OpenFable exists to fill that gap.

---

## What We Believe

**Trust is earned, not licensed.** A verification layer should be open, auditable, and accessible — not a premium feature.

**The best AI is the AI you can actually use.** Infrastructure shouldn't be a barrier. Models shouldn't require a GPU farm. Verified output shouldn't require an enterprise agreement.

**Open source is the foundation.** We build on the work of KingLabsA, the FableForge ecosystem, and the broader open-source AI community. What we add is the service layer — the infrastructure, the reliability, the experience.

---

## What We're Building

A hosted verification engine. Silent critique rounds on every generation. Catching bugs, fixing logic, refining tone — before the user ever sees the output.

The user doesn't see the machinery. They just get better results.
